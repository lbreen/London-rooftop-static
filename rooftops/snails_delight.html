About this rooftop

London's best French rooftop, serving cheese and snail platters all night. This bar offers incredible views of Canary Wharf, with an equally stunning atmosphere. Snail's Delight is a particularly popular destination with the local workers in the City.

Type of drinks: French Free wifi 2 mins from South Quay DLR station or 5 mins from Canary Wharf tube station. Max occupancy: 150
